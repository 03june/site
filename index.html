<This website is a collection of a series of online lectures by Rietlanden Women's Office, Diorama Type Partners and Velvetyne Type Foundry. The speakers discuss neutrality, legibility and readability in typography. Each lecture script includes personal reflections and notes. I invite you to think about what neutrality in typography means for you. Consider how the design choices we make can impact the way information is perceived and understood. Explore how different typefaces and layouts can influence the readability and accessibility of text, and reflect on the role of personal bias in typographic decisions. By engaging with these lectures you will gain insight into some of the designers' work and learn what they think about neutrality./>
